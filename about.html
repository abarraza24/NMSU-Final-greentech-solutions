About GreenTech Solutions

GreenTech Solutions is a small eco-tech startup based in Albuquerque, New Mexico. We're a subsidiary of GreenChile LLC, a local company that's been around the sustainability space for a while. We focus on the tech side devices, software, and the kind of practical stuff offices actually use.

Our Story

GreenTech started in 2022 because our founder, David Ramirez, got tired of watching his coworkers throw aluminum cans, paper, and water bottles straight into the regular trash. The recycling bin was right there. Like, three feet away. People just didn't want to think about it.

David figured if a recycling bin sitting next to a trash can wasn't enough to change behavior, maybe the tools needed to change. So he started building smarter bins, energy monitors, and eventually carbon tracking software, all under the GreenChile LLC umbrella here in New Mexico.

A few years later, GreenTech is a small team helping offices across the state cut waste, lower energy bills, and stop pretending their recycling habits are better than they actually are.
