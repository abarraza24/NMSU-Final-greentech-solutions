Available Solutions

Smart Energy Monitor

Plugs in and shows you exactly where your energy is going. Most offices find out within the first week that something they never thought about is running 24/7.

Smart Recycling Station

A bin that does the sorting so your team doesn't have to. Tracks how much you actually divert from the trash, which is usually a humbling number at first.

Carbon Tracking Dashboard

Web-based dashboard that connects to your utility accounts and devices. It tells you your emissions in plain numbers, not the kind of jargon that needs a degree to read.

Efficient Lighting Upgrade

We come out, swap your old fixtures for LED with motion sensors, and you stop paying to light empty conference rooms at 8pm. Usually pays for itself in about two years.

Office Sustainability Audit

A walkthrough of your space where we find the obvious waste, the not-so-obvious waste, and the stuff you can fix this week without buying anything from us.

Reporting and Analytics Tool

Generates the kind of clean PDF reports you'd need for an ESG filing, a grant application, or a client that suddenly wants your emissions data by Friday.
